~Who is mama?~

Mama or otherwise known as Haidee E,Saberit is the owner of born at october 2nd of 1978 in caloocan and later moved to quezon city and finished her college their and later decided to move to japan to work, Mama's milktea shop located in ibarakiken japan the shop first started as an portable shop that travels to places where filipino gather's in japan and sell "kakanin" but after a few years decided to invest into starting a bussiness the first shop is currently located in kopo konotori b4 jososhi ibrakiken japan, the first plan is to open an cafe that sell's her trademark "kakanin" but she later changed her mind due to people suddenly having a change in preference which was milktea a tea that has milk and usally serve cold with pearls witch boombed during mid 2019.

Mama's milktea goal is to be a wildly known shop for it's food quality and its authentic taste and to spread the filipino desert's and its signiture milktea while not losing the filipino characteristics even if it grows and change, while staying true to its filipino culture Mama's milktea doesnt discriminate other cultures food so in the near future we plan to have more than just filipino dishes it also plan's to have other culture dishes but having a filipino twist to it so that it can alure more individuals to taste and experince it, the Mama's milktea want it's customer to feel welcome and special when they enter the shop to make sure the people wouldn't just think of it as an shop but as an extention to their home, Mama want to make them feel relaxed and comfortable as a place to do their work or to spend their free time.
